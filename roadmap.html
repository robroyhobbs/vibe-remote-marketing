0–30 天

Use the first month to learn faster than you grow.

把第一个月用来加速学习，而不是盲目扩张。

The first month decides whether this becomes a real product or just a clever launch. Optimize for activation, repeat use, and honest feedback loops.

第一个月决定它会变成真实产品，还是停留在一次聪明的发布。核心是优化激活、复访使用和诚实反馈循环。

If launch posts underperform
如果首发帖表现一般

100 views on X or HN is normal. The fix is serialization + distribution.

X 或 HN 只有 100 次浏览很正常。解决办法是内容序列化 + 主动分发。

For a zero-audience product, X and HN are amplifiers, not the engine. Do not wait for one post to work. Turn the best angle into a series, then manually send it into the right conversations and communities.

对于一个几乎没有受众的新产品，X 和 HN 更像放大器，而不是发动机。不要指望一条帖子自动起飞。把最有反应的角度做成系列内容，再主动送进合适的对话和社区里。

Serialized

内容序列化

Take the best-performing angle and turn it into 5–10 smaller posts, clips, screenshots, and founder notes. One launch post is not a launch.

把反馈最好的角度拆成 5–10 条更小的帖子、视频片段、截图和创始人笔记。单条首发内容不叫发布。

Sent

主动送达

After posting, manually distribute it into niche communities, relevant comment threads, private groups, and founder DMs. Distribution is a second job, not an afterthought.

发完之后，要主动分发到垂直社区、相关评论区、私域群和创始人私信里。分发是第二份工作，不是顺手一发。

Measured

持续衡量

Track not just views, but installs, successful pairings, replies, DMs, calls, and repeat visits. Conversation quality matters more than raw reach.

不要只看浏览量，要看安装、成功配对、回复、私信、访谈和回访。对话质量比表面曝光更重要。

14-day exposure plan
14 天曝光计划

What to do after the first weak launch post

首发帖反应一般之后，具体该怎么做

Days 1–3 — Find the winning angle

第 1–3 天——找出最有反应的角度

  • Pick the one post or community angle that got the strongest reactions, not the most likes.
  • Turn it into 3 short X posts, 1 reply thread, 1 HN comment update, and 1 screenshot post.
  • Focus the message on one pain: “SSH from phone feels like a workaround.”
  • 挑出反应最强的那个角度，不看点赞，看回复质量。
  • 把它拆成 3 条短帖、1 条回复串、1 条 HN 评论更新和 1 条截图帖。
  • 文案只聚焦一个痛点：“手机 SSH 像 workaround，不像产品。”

Days 4–7 — Manually send it

第 4–7 天——主动送出去

  • Post tailored versions to V2EX, r/ClaudeAI, Indie Hackers, and relevant X replies.
  • DM 10–15 people who already talk about Claude Code, Codex, remote workflows, or on-call debugging.
  • Ask for one thing only: “Would you try this and tell me what feels confusing or sketchy?”
  • 把定制版本发到 V2EX、r/ClaudeAI、Indie Hackers，以及相关 X 评论串。
  • 私信 10–15 位已经在聊 Claude Code、Codex、远程工作流或线上问题处理的人。
  • 只求一件事：“你愿意试一下，并告诉我哪里让你困惑或不放心吗？”

Days 8–10 — Publish proof, not claims

第 8–10 天——发布证据，而不是口号

  • Share a short clip, a real workflow story, and 3 things users told you.
  • Post one “what we changed this week” update to show the product is alive.
  • Reuse exact user language in the next round of posts.
  • 发一个短视频、一个真实使用故事，以及 3 条用户原话。
  • 发布一条“这周我们改了什么”的更新，让人知道产品在迭代。
  • 下一轮文案直接复用用户的原话。

Days 11–14 — Double down on what converts

第 11–14 天——加码有效内容

  • Repeat the top angle with a new proof asset instead of inventing a new message.
  • Keep a simple scorecard: views, replies, installs, pairings, calls, repeat users.
  • Drop channels that bring empty traffic and keep channels that create conversations.
  • 拿最有效的角度继续打，但换新的证据素材，不要重新发明一套新口号。
  • 只记录几个核心数：浏览、回复、安装、配对、访谈、回访。
  • 放弃只带来空流量的渠道，保留能带来对话的渠道。
0–30 day operating plan
0–30 天执行计划

Use the first month to learn fast, not scale early

第一个月的目标是快速学习，而不是过早放量

Week 1 — Assets + instrumentation

第 1 周——资产 + 埋点

  • Finish demo video and core screenshots.
  • Add install → pair → first prompt tracking.
  • Prepare founder posts and community angles.
  • DM 10–15 trusted developers for pre-launch feedback.
  • 完成 demo 视频和核心截图。
  • 打通安装 → 配对 → 第一条提示词的数据链路。
  • 准备创始人帖子与社区角度。
  • 私信 10–15 位可信开发者做发布前反馈。

Week 2 — Launch + conversations

第 2 周——发布 + 对话

  • Launch across HN, V2EX, X, r/ClaudeAI, Product Hunt.
  • Reply to every comment and DM.
  • Book 5–10 short calls with installers and hesitant users.
  • Ship one “what we learned” update within 72 hours.
  • 在 HN、V2EX、X、r/ClaudeAI、Product Hunt 同步发布。
  • 回复每一条评论和私信。
  • 约 5–10 个短访谈，覆盖安装用户和犹豫用户。
  • 72 小时内发布一篇“我们学到了什么”。

Week 3 — Activation + proof

第 3 周——激活 + 证据

  • Fix onboarding friction from real support questions.
  • Add a “best first 3 prompts” flow to docs/app.
  • Capture screenshots, testimonials, and short case studies.
  • Start lightweight email capture for interested visitors.
  • 根据真实支持问题修正 onboarding 阻力。
  • 给文档 / App 加上“最佳前 3 条提示词”。
  • 沉淀截图、评价和短案例。
  • 开始轻量级邮件收集，留住潜在用户。

Week 4 — First paid conversion

第 4 周——首个付费转化

  • Identify heavy users and frequent returners.
  • Ask what would make PRO worth paying for.
  • Personally support the first 3 likely buyers.
  • Document the full first-payment story.
  • 识别重度用户和高频回访用户。
  • 直接询问：什么会让 PRO 值得付费？
  • 亲自服务前 3 个潜在付费用户。
  • 完整记录第一笔付款的故事。
Feedback engine
反馈引擎

Ask better questions

用更好的问题收集反馈

  • What almost stopped you? This surfaces trust and setup blockers.
  • What did you expect to happen? This surfaces messaging mismatch.
  • What was the first valuable moment? This surfaces activation hooks.
  • What would make this a must-have? This surfaces the path to payment.
  • What workaround would you go back to without this? This surfaces real replacement behavior.
  • 什么差点阻止你使用？ 找出信任和连接阻力。
  • 你原本预期会发生什么？ 找出文案与真实体验的偏差。
  • 第一个有价值的时刻是什么？ 找出激活钩子。
  • 什么会让它变成必备工具？ 找出付费路径。
  • 如果没有它，你会回到什么方案？ 找出真实替代关系。
Founder loop
创始人循环

In zero-to-one, conversations are a core metric.

在 0 到 1 阶段，“和用户聊了多少”本身就是核心指标。

  • Talk to installers and non-installers every week.
  • Turn exact user language into homepage and App Store copy.
  • Publish what you changed because of user feedback.
  • Track repeated objections like product bugs: fix or explain them.
  • 每周都聊安装用户和未安装用户。
  • 把用户原话直接写进官网和 App Store 文案。
  • 公开你因为用户反馈而做的改变。
  • 把高频异议当成产品 bug 一样处理：要么修复，要么解释清楚。
